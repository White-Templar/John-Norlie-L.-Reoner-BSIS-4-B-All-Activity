This folder contains custom fonts for the medical appointment app.

To use custom fonts, download and add the following files:

Roboto Font Family:
- Roboto-Regular.ttf
- Roboto-Bold.ttf
- Roboto-Light.ttf
- Roboto-Medium.ttf

Poppins Font Family:
- Poppins-Regular.ttf
- Poppins-Bold.ttf
- Poppins-Light.ttf
- Poppins-Medium.ttf

Download from:
- Google Fonts (https://fonts.google.com/)
- Font Squirrel (https://www.fontsquirrel.com/)

These fonts are already configured in pubspec.yaml and used throughout the app.
